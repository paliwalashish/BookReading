# Chapter 04: Creating your working draft

The chapter covers details on how to create working draft, outlining process and helpful tips to getting started writing.

The chapter covers the ways/tips to create first draft
- Focus and challenges
  - Get started rather than making it perfect
- Initial Prep
  - researching about topic sentiments
- Optional warmup
  - Outlining
    - creating a skeleton
  - Mindmapping
  - Working from a model article
- Writing time
  - Start filing the paragraphs
  - Eliminate blockers
    - Resist perfection
    - Drop placeholder when stuck
- Filling the gaps
  - validate you cover what you intended to
